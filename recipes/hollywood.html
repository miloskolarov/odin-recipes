Hollywood Diet

Description

Plain and boiled chicken breast, broccoli, and rice.

In order for your meal to be as plain as possible avoid adding any spices.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Chicken
  • Broccoli
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken until it's done
  2. Boil the broccoli until it's done
  3. Boil the rice until it's done
  4. Enjoy (I guess)
